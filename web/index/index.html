About Snapp!
Snapp! turns Snap! projects into standalone executables!

If you ended up here and don't know about Snap! go check it out at http://snap.berkeley.edu/
Snapp! was developed in 2015 by Adrian Hintze as his end of course project under the supervision of Jordi Delgado (FIB-UPC) and Bernat Romagosa (Edutec-Citilab)

Snapp! is open source and is available at GitHub

If you have any suggestions or find any issues with the application you can contact the author at adrian.hintze AT est.fib.upc.edu
